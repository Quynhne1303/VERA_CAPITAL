Kinh Bac City Development Holding Corporation (KBC)

Financial Goals for Upcoming Years

Rental Area in 2025: KBC aims to achieve over 200 hectares from industrial parks (IPs) such as Nam Son Hap Linh, Tan Phu Trung, Hung Yen Industrial Cluster, and Trang Due 3 IP.
Revenue: To be generated from social housing projects in Nenh Town and Trang Due Urban Area.
Business Targets for 2025: Consolidated revenue of VND 10,000 billion and after-tax profit of VND 3,200 billion, nearly 7 times higher than in 2024, reflecting strong growth expectations.

Financial Ratios (2020-2024)

P/E Ratio: High and volatile (ranging from 10.72 to 51.18)
EPS: Unstable (ranging from VND 0.48 to VND 2.65).
ROE: Fluctuating (2.1% to 10.67%).
D/A Ratio: Decreasing, safer than the industry average.

Strengths

Large land bank with strategic locations and a reputable brand.
Strong ability to attract foreign direct investment (FDI).
Safe financial structure with a low D/A ratio.
High P/E ratio, indicating market confidence.

Risks

Volatile profits, heavily dependent on project progress.
Exposure to macroeconomic factors (FDI, real estate cycles).
Pressure to maintain growth to meet market expectations.
Low D/A ratio may limit leverage opportunities.

Financial Goals for Upcoming Years

2025 Targets: Achieve a profit of VND 2,400 billion and increase energy capacity by 100 MW, focusing on renewable energy. Office rental revenue is expected to reach VND 1,267 billion, up 18.5%.

Financial Ratios (2020-2024)

P/E Ratio: Decreased from 2020 to 2022, then rose sharply to 15.2 in 2024, higher than the industry average.

Strengths

Historically strong operational efficiency (higher ROE and EPS than the industry).
Safe financial structure with low debt.
Growth potential in renewable energy and real estate.

Risks

Declining efficiency in 2024 (lower ROE and EPS).
Sensitivity to macroeconomic factors (interest rates, inflation) and intense competition.
